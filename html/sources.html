“Population, Total | Data.” Worldbank.Org, 2019, data.worldbank.org/indicator/SP.POP.TOTL. ‌

I trust that this is a legitimate source because it comes from a well-known organization that has a list of their complied sources that they used to develop their data. The population in a country is important for my data for my project because it is helpful to show exports, imports, production, and consumption in proportion to a country's population. Usually, a country with a larger population will consume more while a country with a smaller population will consume less.
